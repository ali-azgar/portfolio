About Project

Rajasthan Royals (often abbreviated as RR) are a franchise cricket team based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008 as one of the initial eight IPL franchises, the team is based at the Sawai Mansingh Stadium in Jaipur.

Problem Statement

  • Low app install rate.
  • Usability issues.
  • High uninstall rate.
  • No exclusive content.
  • Less active users.
  • Ineffective gaming features.
  • Crash issues.
  • App is slow.

Business Goals - RR needs to enhance their web & mobile user experiences intended to;

  • Improve fan loyalty.
  • Improved fan-player connectivity.
  • Increase user engagement for website and the mobile app.
  • Increase conversion rate for both website and mobile app.
  • Increase mobile app download rate.
  • Sell more merchandise.
  • Increase leads for Royal business school and Royals Academy.

Possible Solution

Engaging UI, Gamification ideas, Research & Strategize product to empower fans engagement through online RR app and website. Where cricket fans can easily track their favorite players, play and win coins and shop within the app using coins.

  • Implement gamification ideas to engage more.
  • Get fans to spend more time on the website and app.
  • Build a loyal community.
  • Increase sales of merchandise.
  • Contribute to society.
  • Sell more merchandise.
  • Promote Foundation, Royals academy, Royal school of business

Engagement Ideas

Micro Intearactions

• Small micro interactions to add delight to the user's experience.
• Give a more modern and responsive experience to the user.

Gamification

• Make games more engaging.
• Fun and easy to play games with high scores.
• Offer rewards like RR coins.

Cricket Manager

• Allow fans to find nearby matches.
• Allow fans to coordinate and play.

Accessibility

• Include fans who speak in Hindi and other local languages.
• Include visually impaired, colorblind, and older fans.

Content

• The app and website to follow similar content strategy.
• Engaging content with broader scope.
• Focus on increasing entertainment and learning value.

Merchandise

• Make branding and visuals more attractive.
• Give more visibility to product photos on homepage.
• Offer customization and signed products.

Fan Bonding

• Allow fans to bond together.
• Comment on the games and players via forums etc.
• Share quiz scores and match predictions.
• Share links from the store.
• Invite friends to play via cricket manager.

Novelty

• Novel, engaging and curated content which resonates with sports fans.
• Monthly update content on a topic form the cricketing world. Curate content and weave RR in and out of the narrative.
• Fulfilling, entertaining, information building for RR fans.

User Persona 1

Tushar Sharma is an IT service provider with an MNC. He spends his day working hard and has demanding work hours. He loves all formats of cricket and has been a Rajasthan Royals fan years.

Pain Points

  • He needs more entertainment after going through SM.
  • He wants to know more about his favourite players off season.
  • He wants to make suggestions about the team.
  • He wants to engage with other fans.
  • He wants to buy team merchandise.

Motivations

  • Vicarious achievement.
  • Entertainment.
  • Bonding and connectedness.
  • Role model.
  • Increasing knowledge.
  • Escape.
